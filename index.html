Experienced Automatic and Manual Transmission Instruction

Our instruction comes from over 18 years of experience as a driver and over 9 years of experience as a Certified CDL License Examiner. We teach you exactly what you need to know to be exceptional at your job and to be successful in your testing. We train our students in a manual 10 speed transmission or automatic. Many trucking schools will train you only in automatic, but we like to give students the option to choose what is best for them and their future career. After training is complete, we test and license our students at our campus.
